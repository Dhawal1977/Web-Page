Shipping Guidelines

Prior to shipping with us, please ensure your packages meet our guidelines for safe and efficient delivery. We recommend using sturdy packaging materials and secure sealing methods. Please be mindful of size and weight limits, and avoid shipping prohibited items such as hazardous materials or illegal substances. Some items may have restrictions or require special handling, so please refer to our guidelines for detailed information. For international shipments, accurate completion of customs documentation is essential to prevent delays. As the packages will be in our possession before shipping, rest assured that our team will handle them with care and attention to detail. Consider purchasing shipping insurance for added protection, and feel free to take advantage of our additional services like package tracking and delivery confirmation for peace of mind throughout the shipping process.
